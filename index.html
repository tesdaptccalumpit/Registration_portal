📖 ABOUT US

PTC Calumpit was established in April 1992 thru Calumpit Sangguniang Bayan Resolution No. 46-92 of LGU-Calumpit in response to the growing needs of industries for skilled manpower in the province.

The land and buildings (except the administration office) is owned and built by the local government. It operates on the strength of a Deed of Usufruct between TESDA-III and LGU-Calumpit. The renewed contract was forged on November 8, 2018. The term is good for 15 years.

Vision

Model Green TVET Training Center in Central Luzon

Mission

Cultivate a culture of innovativeness and sustainable practices within our institution and community in providing high-quality technical and vocational education and training.

Goal

  • Sustainable development and renewable energy-themed programs and projects
  • To produce highly competitive, innovative and industry-ready graduates
